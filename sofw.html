LeadMe - Software Design

The LeadMe app gets you started quickly, with a map showing your location and the option to search for a route. Once you've found where you're going, the app seamlessly gives turn by turn navigation onscreen and through vibrations in the anklet.

Design

LeadMe's Android app connects the physical anklet to an easy to use interface. Turn by turn navigation, suggested search autocomplete, and easy configuration gets you moving without a hassle.

Start screen

Upon launch, the map centers on your current location and lets you browse nearby or search for your final destination.

Search interface

Know where you're going? No problem! Search autocomplete lets you find what you're looking for quickly and effortlessly.

Route display

Once you've found where you're headed, the app shows you the quickest way to get there.

Turn-by-turn navigation

With your destination locked down, put your phone in your pocket. The anklet vibrates with your directions. Still not sure which street to take? The turn-by-turn display helps you find the exact directions you need.
