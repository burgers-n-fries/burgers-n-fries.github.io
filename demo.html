LeadMe

An incredibly simple way of getting around.

What started out as a simple idea to help people get around turned into our 2014 Principles of Engineering (PoE) project. We built an anklet system that a user could use as a guide to their destination, without glancing at their smartphone. To accomplish this, we developed a Mechanical System to house the integrated Electrical System in a comortable form factor ready for any activity. From there, the anklet would be paired by Bluetooth to an Android smartphone, where the LeadMe app could accept destinations and begin travel.
