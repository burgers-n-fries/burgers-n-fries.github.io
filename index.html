LeadMe

An incredibly simple way of getting around.

What started out as a simple idea to help people get around turned into a semester-long 2014 Principles of Engineering (PoE) project at Olin College. We built an anklet system to simply and gently guide anyone to where they want to go, without glancing at their smartphone. To accomplish this, we developed a beautiful device to house the onboard electronics in a comortable form factor ready for any activity. From there, the anklet connects over Bluetooth to any Android smartphone, where the LeadMe app helps users find their destinations and begin travel.
