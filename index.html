LeadMe

An incredibly simple way of getting around.

LeadMe was designed to easily guide you anywhere, without needing to stare at your smartphone. The final product is a carefully designed anklet to house the onboard electronics in a comortable form factor ready to take you wherever you want to go.

The anklet connects over Bluetooth to any Android smartphone, where the LeadMe app helps you find your destination and start moving toward your goals.
